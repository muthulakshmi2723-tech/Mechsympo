🔧 Mechanical Skills & Assembly Challenge

A hands-on technical event that tests participants’ practical knowledge of mechanical components and assembly. Participants must identify, handle, and assemble engine parts accurately within a given time, showcasing their technical skills, precision, and problem-solving ability in real-world mechanical applications.

👥 Individual Participant
🏆 Prize Pool - ₹1300

📌 Prerequisites

    • Participants should have basic knowledge of engine components and working principles
    • Familiarity with parts like piston, crankshaft, valves, cylinder head, etc., is required
    • Understanding of assembly sequence and tool handling is recommended
    • Participants must be comfortable using basic mechanical tools safely
    • Basic troubleshooting and identification skills will be an added advantage
    • Participants must report on time with proper registration

📜 Rules

    • Only individual participation is allowed
    • The disassembled engine components will be provided at the venue
    • Participants must assemble the engine within the given time limit
    • Proper sequence and correct fitting of components are mandatory
    • Points will be awarded based on accuracy, time taken, and assembly quality
    • Participants must use only the tools provided (or as instructed)
    • Any damage to components due to improper handling may lead to penalty
    • Seeking help from others or using external aids is strictly prohibited
    • Incomplete or incorrect assembly will result in lower scores or disqualification
    • Judges’ decision will be final and binding

📤 General Instructions

    • Safety precautions must be followed throughout the event
    • Participants should maintain discipline and follow coordinator instructions
