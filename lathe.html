Mr. LATHE

⚙️ Machining & Precision Challenge

A practical technical event that tests participants’ skills in operating conventional lathe machines. Participants must perform machining operations such as turning, facing, and step cutting with high accuracy within a limited time, demonstrating their precision, workmanship, and knowledge of machining processes.

👥 Individual Participant
🏆 Prize Pool - ₹1300

📌 Prerequisites

    • Participants must have basic knowledge of Lathe operations
    • Only conventional lathe machines will be used
    • Participants must bring their own safety equipment (apron, shoes, etc., if available); if not, it will be provided, but shoes are mandatory
    • Basic tools and raw materials will be provided or informed in advance
    • Participants must report to the venue before the allotted time
    • Participants must ensure they understand machine safety and operation procedures

📜 Rules

    • Participation should be individual
    • Participants will be given 3 designs and can choose any one
    • The design and time limit will be announced on the spot
    • Participants must complete the given machining work within the allotted time
    • Only lathe operations are allowed (turning, facing, step turning, etc.)
    • Proper safety measures must be followed strictly
    • Evaluation will be based on accuracy, surface finish, dimensional tolerance, and time management
    • Any damage to the machine or unsafe handling will lead to disqualification
    • Incomplete work may result in lower marks or disqualification
    • The decision of the judges will be final and binding
