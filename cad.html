CAD MASTER

🖥️ Design & Modelling Challenge

A technical event that tests participants’ skills in CAD software by solving real-time design challenges. Participants must create accurate 2D sketches and 3D models within a limited time, showcasing their creativity, precision, and problem-solving ability in mechanical design.

⏱ 30 - 40 min
👥 Individual Participant
🏆 Prize Pool - ₹1300

📌 Prerequisites

    • Participants should have basic knowledge of CAD software (e.g., SolidWorks, AutoCAD, Fusion 360, CATIA)
    • Required software must be installed and tested before the event (if personal laptops are allowed)
    • Familiarity with 2D sketching and 3D modelling concepts is essential
    • Participants must bring necessary accessories (mouse, charger, etc.) if using personal devices
    • Understanding of dimensioning, constraints, and assembly concepts is recommended
    • All participants must report on time with their registration details

📜 Rules

    • Only individual participation is allowed (team entries are not permitted)
    • The modelling task/problem statement will be given on the spot
    • Time limit will be specified (e.g., 30–40 minutes) and must be strictly followed
    • Participants must create the model using only CAD software (no external help allowed)
    • Internet usage or reference materials may be restricted during the event
    • Evaluation will be based on accuracy, dimensions, design approach, and completeness
    • Any form of malpractice or copying will lead to immediate disqualification
    • Judges’ decision will be final and binding

📤 Submission

Models must be submitted within the allotted time
