PROJECT EXPO

🚀 Innovation & Model Showcase

A dynamic technical event where participants present their innovative projects and working models based on mechanical engineering concepts. This event provides a platform to demonstrate creativity, practical knowledge, and real-world problem-solving skills through project explanation and live demonstration.

👥 Individual or a Team(MAX 4 members)
🏆 Prize Pool - ₹1300

📌 Prerequisites

    • Participants must prepare their project/model in advance
    • The project topic must be finalized before the event
    • Teams must have a team name and mention it clearly in the project display
    • Individual participants must mention their name clearly on the project
    • All required components such as extension box, wires, tools, and other materials must be brought by the participants themselves
    • Working models (if applicable) must be fully functional before presentation

📜 Rules

    • Participation can be individual or a team (maximum 4 members)
    • The project must be related to Mechanical Engineering concepts
    • Participants must be ready to explain their project within the given time limit
    • If it is a working model, participants must demonstrate its working during evaluation
    • Projects must be original; copied ideas or plagiarism will lead to disqualification
    • Participants are responsible for the safety and proper handling of their models and equipment
    • Judges may ask questions, and participants must be able to clearly justify their project
    • Late entries or incomplete setups will not be accepted
    • The decision of the judges will be final and binding
