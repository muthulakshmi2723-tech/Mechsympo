NON-TECHNICAL EVENT

TREASURE HUNT

🗺️ Decode & Discover

An exciting and adventurous event where teams solve clues and complete challenges to uncover hidden treasures. Participants must use their logical thinking, teamwork, and problem-solving skills to follow a sequence of clues, navigate through different locations, and reach the final destination within the given time.

👥 Team (Max 4 - 5 members)
🏆 Prize Pool - ₹1000

📜 Rules

    • Participation must be in teams of 4–5 members.
    • Each team must register with a unique team name before the event.
    • Clues will be given on the spot and must be followed in sequence.
    • Teams must solve each clue to unlock the next stage.
    • The hunt will take place within the college campus and surrounding areas only.
    • Teams must complete the hunt within the given time limit.
    • Use of mobile phones or external help is not allowed unless specified.
    • Teams must follow all instructions and designated paths during the hunt.
    • Any form of malpractice, misbehavior, or damage to property will lead to disqualification.
    • The first team to successfully reach the final point/treasure wins.
    • In case of a tie, the team with better time performance will be considered.
    • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
