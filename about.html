About Mecnovate' 26

Mecnovate' 26 is a national-level technical symposium organized by the Department of Mechanical Engineering, aimed at fostering innovation, technical excellence, and creative thinking among students. The symposium provides a dynamic platform for participants to showcase their talents through a wide range of technical and non-technical events, encouraging knowledge sharing and practical learning. It brings together aspiring engineers from various institutions to collaborate, compete, and explore emerging technologies in the field of mechanical engineering. The event is guided and coordinated under the leadership of Dr. V. Manieniyan, Symposium Coordinator, whose dedication and vision play a vital role in making a successful and impactful event.
