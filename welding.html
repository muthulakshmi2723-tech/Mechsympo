TECHNICAL EVENT

WELDING SKILLS

🔥 Precision & Fabrication Challenge

A hands-on technical event focused on testing participants’ welding skills using the Arc Welding process. Participants must create accurate weld joints based on given designs within a specified time, showcasing their precision, workmanship, and adherence to safety standards in fabrication.

👥 Individual Participant
🏆 Prize Pool - ₹1300

📌 Prerequisites

    • Participants must have basic knowledge of Arc Welding
    • Only Manual Metal Arc Welding (MMAW) process will be used
    • Participants must bring their own safety equipment such as gloves, apron, and safety shoes (if available); if not, it will be provided, but leather shoes are mandatory
    • All required materials like electrodes and basic tools will be provided
    • Participants must report to the venue before the allotted time
    • Each participant/team must register their name and details clearly before the event begins

📜 Rules

    • Participation should be individual
    • The event is strictly based on Arc Welding only
    • Participants will be given more than 4 design options, and they can choose any two designs
    • The design and time limit will be announced on the spot
    • Participants must complete the selected design within the given time
    • Proper safety procedures must be followed during welding
    • Judges will evaluate based on weld quality, accuracy, finishing, and strength
    • Any unsafe practice or misconduct will lead to disqualification
    • Incomplete work within the given time may lead to lower evaluation or disqualification
    • The decision of the judges will be final and binding
