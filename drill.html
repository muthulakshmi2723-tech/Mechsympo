🛠️ Accuracy & Machining Challenge

A hands-on technical event that evaluates participants’ skills in drilling operations using standard machines. Participants must perform precise drilling tasks based on given patterns within a limited time, demonstrating their accuracy, alignment, measurement skills, and safe handling of machining tools.

👥 Individual Participant
🏆 Prize Pool - ₹1300

📌 Prerequisites

    • Participants must have basic knowledge of drilling operations
    • Only standard drilling machines will be used
    • Participants must bring their own safety equipment (gloves, apron, shoes, if available)
    • Required tools and materials will be provided or informed earlier
    • Participants must report to the venue on time
    • Participants must be aware of drilling safety practices

📜 Rules

    • Participation should be individual
    • Participants will be given more than 3 design/pattern options
    • The design and time limit will be announced on the spot
    • Participants must complete the drilling work within the given time
    • Only drilling-related operations are allowed (drilling, reaming, etc., if specified)
    • Proper alignment and measurement must be maintained
    • Evaluation will be based on accuracy, hole alignment, finish, and completion time
    • Any unsafe practice or improper machine handling will lead to disqualification
    • Incomplete work may lead to lower marks or disqualification
    • The decision of the judges will be final and binding
