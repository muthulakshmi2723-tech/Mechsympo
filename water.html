NON-TECHNICAL EVENT

WATER ROCKETRY

🚀 Build & Launch

A thrilling technical event where participants design and launch their own water-powered rockets. Using principles of physics and creativity, teams must build efficient and safe rockets powered by water and air pressure, aiming to achieve maximum height, distance, and stability during launch.

👥 Team of 2 Members
🏆 Prize Pool - ₹800

📜 Rules

    • Participation should be in teams of two members.
    • Participants must design and build their own water rocket.
    • Only water and air pressure should be used as the propellant (no chemicals allowed).
    • Participants must bring all required materials and equipment on their own.
    • The rocket must be safe and properly assembled before launch.
    • Launch will be conducted only in the designated area.
    • Each team will be given limited attempts as decided by the organizers.
    • Evaluation will be based on height, distance, and stability of the rocket.
    • Any unsafe design or practice will lead to disqualification.
    • Participants must follow all instructions given by the coordinators.
    • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
