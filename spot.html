NON-TECHNICAL EVENT

SPOT ACTING

🎭 Act & Express

A lively and entertaining event where participants perform spontaneous acting based on given situations or characters. Participants must showcase their creativity, expression, and acting skills using only gestures and body language, making the performance engaging, realistic, and impactful within the given time.

👥 Team of 2 Members
🏆 Prize Pool - ₹800

📜 Rules

    • Participation should be in teams of two members.
    • Participants must design and build their own water rocket.
    • Only water and air pressure should be used as the propellant (no chemicals allowed).
    • Participants must bring all required materials and equipment on their own.
    • The rocket must be safe and properly assembled before launch.
    • Launch will be conducted only in the designated area.
    • Participation is strictly in teams of 2 members.
    • Each team will be given 5 famous characters on the spot.
    • One participant must act, and the other participant must guess the character.
    • The acting must be done without speaking dialogues (only expressions and actions).
    • Teams must complete the task within the given time limit.
    • The goal is to identify all characters as quickly as possible.
    • The team that correctly guesses all characters in the shortest time will be declared the winner.
    • Any form of hints using words, mobile phones, or external help is not allowed.
    • Participants must maintain fair play and discipline during the event.
    • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
